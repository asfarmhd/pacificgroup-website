Leadership

Decades of experience and a shared vision for growth.

Our leadership brings decades of experience, industry expertise, and a shared vision for sustainable growth across Pakistan.

Mr. Ghulam Mustafa

Mr. Ghulam Mustafa is a seasoned professional businessman with 40 years of experience in managing and maintaining a logistics fleet of over 100 vehicles. Handling day to day operations amicably, he was able to build trust among clients that have remained with the group over the past two decades. He has strong ties with the community and is actively involved in philanthropical works.

Mr. Bilal Mustafa

Mr. Bilal Mustafa is a young and dynamic entrepreneur. He is one of the key directors of the group and has established himself as a seasoned veteran of the palm oil industry with an experience of more than 15 years. Mr. Bilal has become a known face in Pakistan's palm oil industry by attending various palm oil conferences and programs, especially in Malaysia. He has a strong vision for growth and continuously strives to place the group as one of the leading business groups in the country expanding its base to various other sectors showing prominent sustainable growth opportunities.

Mr. Daniyal Mustafa

Mr. Daniyal Mustafa did bachelor's in accounting and finance from the University of Warwick, UK and further did MBA in 2020. After moving back to Pakistan in 2017, he joined the group and introduced various new systems in the organization related to logistics management and Finance. Bringing his innovative mind to the industry, he developed fleets that catered to the specific demands of the clients and established work practices in the Logistics sector that meet Global Health and Safety standards.
